## Sources
Youtube was one of the best source for me to help me decide to go with materialize as my tool for my freedom project. These videos helped me visually understand how materialize works and the codes for it, along with explaination provided by the youtuber. There were videos such as the [crash course](https://youtu.be/nqT8c5OFjEQ?si=JUg2NAl4y1H500nZ) and the individual [mini lessons](https://youtu.be/gCZ3y6mQpW0?si=zZ5h_Vz75Q2o_8oq) that are about 3-5 long explaining the different ways to work with materialize.
